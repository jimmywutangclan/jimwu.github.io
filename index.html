My name is Jim Wu

I am a student in Computer Science and Game Development at Northeastern University, and I kind of got here because this major was the perfect synergy of two things I've always loved, electronics and games.

From gaming, I then got hooked onto the PC through Roblox, and long story short, I'm who I am now.

Nowadays, I'm just a compsci student, having recently concluded a co-op at Mythics Inc. as a DevOps engineer. I'm going back to school this semester, ready to learn about computer graphics, computer networks, and game engine development.
